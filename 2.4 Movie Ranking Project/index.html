My Top 3 Movies For All Time

3 idiots

The story explores the pressures of the education system, friendship, and the pursuit of passion over societal expectations. Rancho, the free-spirited and curious student, inspires others to question conventional norms and follow their hearts.

Kalki

Kalki 2898 AD is a futuristic sci-fi action film inspired by Hindu mythology, set in a dystopian world where darkness and corruption reign. The story revolves around the emergence of Kalki, the tenth and final avatar of Lord Vishnu, destined to restore balance and destroy evil in the modern age. With breathtaking visuals, advanced technology, and mythological depth, it blends ancient prophecies with a high-tech, action-packed narrative.

10Th Fail

10th Fail is an inspiring biographical drama based on the true story of Manoj Kumar Sharma, a young boy from a small town who failed his 10th-grade exams but refused to give up on his dreams. Despite poverty, setbacks, and societal pressure, he overcomes challenges to become a respected IPS officer. The film is a tribute to resilience, self-belief, and the power of education.

Sita Ramam

Sita Ramam is a poetic romantic drama set against the backdrop of war and national duty. It tells the story of Lieutenant Ram, a selfless Indian army officer stationed at the Kashmir border, who receives anonymous love letters from a woman named Sita. As their emotional bond grows, a tale of love, sacrifice, honor, and identity unfolds, connecting the lives of several people across timelines.

Avengers Endgame

Avengers: Endgame is the epic conclusion to Marvel’s Infinity Saga, where Earth’s mightiest heroes reunite to undo the destruction caused by Thanos in Infinity War. The Avengers embark on a time-traveling mission to retrieve the Infinity Stones and restore balance to the universe. It’s a powerful story of sacrifice, teamwork, closure, and hope, packed with emotional depth, action, and legacy moments.
