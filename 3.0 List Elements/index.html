Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter (melted)

Instructions

  1. Mix the warm milk with the yeast and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the egg and egg yolk to the mixture.
  3. Melt the butter and add it to the mixture.
  4. Gradually add the bread flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  5. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth.
  6. Transfer the dough to a large bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for 2 hours or until doubled in size.
  7. After rising, roll out the dough into a large rectangle on a floured surface.
  8. For the filling, melt the butter and mix it with dark brown sugar and ground cinnamon.
  9. Spread the filling evenly over the rolled-out dough.
  10. Roll the dough tightly into a Swiss roll shape.
  11. Cut the roll into 3 cm sections and place them flat in a greased baking tray.
  12. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  13. Bake the rolls for 20-25 minutes or until lightly browned.
  14. Let them cool slightly before serving.
